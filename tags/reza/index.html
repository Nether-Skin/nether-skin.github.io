  • The law-abiding abyss

    Federico Nieto
    Jan 27, 2023 theory reza

    The following essay intends to address the idea of a ‘radical discontinuity’ appearing in philosopher Reza Negarestani’s development from an early theory-fictional oriented work represented in Cyclonopedia (2008), to the recent synthesis of continental and contemporary analytical philosophical perspectives found in Intelligence and Spirit (2018). With this in mind, the idea of such a ‘radical discontinuity’ in Negarestani’s work will be contested by proposing a possible continuum in Negarestani’s writing between Cyclonopedia and Intelligence and Spirit found in the explicit and implicit utilization of concepts such as: ‘the inhuman’ and ‘the Outside’.
